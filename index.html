About

Xero-Certified Bookkeeper | Helping Australian Businesses Stay Compliant, Profitable & Stress-Free

Hi, I’m Zab—a detail-driven, results-focused bookkeeper with over 6 years of experience supporting Australian businesses across industries including construction, childcare, NDIS, retail, and education.

I specialise in Xero bookkeeping, payroll, and BAS preparation—ensuring your books are clean, compliant, and always ATO-ready.

Whether you’re just starting out, catching up on overdue books, or scaling your business, I help transform financial chaos into clear, confident numbers. I use tools like Xero, Hubdoc, DEXT, XPM, and more to streamline your processes and take the pressure off your plate.

My clients describe me as reliable, accurate, and easy to work with—because I genuinely care about your success. If you're ready to get your books in order and reclaim your time, let’s chat.
